FUNDRAISING DOUGHNUTS

The sweetest way to raise money

If you're looking for a unique fundraising idea to raise money for a charity close to your heart, then why not take a look at ordering some Fundraising doughnuts from Krispy Kreme - it's easy, fun and our doughnuts always sell quickly, meaning you'll have a successful Fundraising day!

Let's get started

FUNDRAISING WITH ORIGINAL GLAZED DOUGHNUTS

Whether you are a charity looking to raise money at a specific event or an individual looking for a different way to hit that sponsorship target, our discounted dozens are a great fundraising idea!

How it works

  • Purchase your Krispy Kreme fundraising doughnuts at a discounted price from the Krispy Kreme website.

  • Collect your doughnuts and sell your freshly made doughnuts at your fundraising event on the day of collection.

  • Send all the money you’ve raised to your chosen charity and tell us much you raised - we'll send you a reminder to let us know!

WHAT NEXT?

First, decide how many doughnuts you need and the date you require them (minimum order is 5 dozen, maximum is 50 dozen per event, per day). We'll need 5 days notice of your fundraising requirements. Next, place your enquiry with us online. Once your order has been successfully processed, collect your doughnuts on the day of your event from your chosen Krispy Kreme UK store. Our doughnuts are always made fresh daily and must be sold on the day of collection.

Let's get started

If you have any questions, please call our Customer Care team on 01489 576100 or email us at customercare@krispykreme.co.uk Please make sure you have read our terms and conditions and that you share nutritional information on the day of your fundraising event.
